Our story

Award-winning, sustainable eyewear with a social purpose

Bird make beautiful, design-led eyewear
using high quality and sustainable
materials.

“Looking good is one thing – but doing good is so much more important.”

For every pair sold we distribute solar light to remote communities in Africa through our Share Your Sun partnership with SolarAid.

A balance of purpose and profit

The first eyewear
b-corp in the UK

In April 2020 we became the first UK eyewear company to become a certified B Corp.
For us this is about making a public statement that we are a force for good.
